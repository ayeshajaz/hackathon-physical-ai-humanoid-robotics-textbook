# Example Scenarios: Humanoid Locomotion Applications

## Scenario 1: Home Assistant Robot

A humanoid robot designed to navigate household environments for cleaning, maintenance, and assistance tasks.

**Locomotion Requirements:**
- Stable bipedal walking for human-like navigation
- Stair climbing capabilities
- Obstacle avoidance in cluttered spaces
- Balance recovery when bumped by furniture or pets

## Scenario 2: Search and Rescue Robot

A humanoid robot designed for disaster response scenarios, navigating rubble and unstable terrain.

**Locomotion Requirements:**
- Adaptive gait for uneven surfaces
- Crawling for tight spaces
- Balance recovery on unstable ground
- Energy-efficient locomotion for extended missions

## Scenario 3: Industrial Maintenance Robot

A humanoid robot designed to perform maintenance tasks in industrial environments.

**Locomotion Requirements:**
- Precise positioning for manipulation tasks
- Stable stance during heavy lifting
- Navigation around machinery and obstacles
- Adaptation to various floor surfaces (metal, concrete, etc.)
